Why College of Computers and Information System is the best:

In recent years, the world has witnessed tremendous and unprecedented developments in the field of communications and information technology. These developments have doubled the responsibility of the faculties of computers and information in preparing and qualifying students to graduate specialists who are able to deal with the technologies of the communications and information revolution. Within the framework of Fayoum University’s philosophy aimed at reaching the model of the future university through the development and modernization of its educational programs in order to reach international academic accreditation standards, the attempts of the Faculty of Computers and Information to develop its study system come and re-evaluating the curricula and courses being studied, adopting the latest educational systems that allow greater student participation, and open up options for students to study courses according to their abilities, capabilities and desires. In this context, the college seeks to implement the credit hour system in force in many large international universities.
