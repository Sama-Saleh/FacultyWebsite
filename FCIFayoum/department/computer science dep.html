About the department


The main interest of the Department of Computer Science is to follow up, understand and develop the scientific concepts behind what computers accomplish and how they accomplish it with knowledge of how computers are built and how they work.
The department is concerned with analyzing, designing and measuring the degree of complexity of algorithms used to solve real problems
The department focuses on artificial intelligence applications in which computers simulate the intelligent behavior of humans in processing information.
The Department of Computer Science is concerned with teaching and conducting research related to courses such as:
